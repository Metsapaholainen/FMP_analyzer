The three steps

  1. Do your homework. Income statement, balance sheet, cash flow — every key ratio shown vs its own 10-year average / high / low. Anomalies are flagged.
  2. Find the moat. Quantitative fingerprint (sustained ROIC, FCF margin, gross-margin stability, capital allocation) plus a sector-specific qualitative checklist.
  3. Margin of safety. Two-stage DCF, FCF/EV cash return, multiples vs own history. Earnings-quality red flags called out separately.
